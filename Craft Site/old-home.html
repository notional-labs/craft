Factions: Join forces with like minded individuals and work as a group to control certain facets of the economy. Factions will also serve to secure and govern the blockchain. Got a problem with with how much pumpkins cost? Gather your faction and discuss internally then send a proposal to the blockchain. Yes thats right you understood me correctly, factions will double as validators!
EXP Token: Governance will no longer depend on your stake. On chain governance will be controlled by a secondary, valueless token. EXP cannot be bought, sold or traded. Only earned by playing the game. Factions with the largest active user bases will have the highest voting power.
Companies: You really like spelunking? Guess what you can build an entire business out of it. Mining operations, farms, animal breeding  and so much more will be enterprises you can control. Own the supply of diamonds and sell them, craft them or upgrade gear for other players. Be careful, this is a free market, anyone can do it better then you.
Stocks: As if trading assets in real life wasn’t enough of a pain, Craft Economy lets you hate your life trading stocks in Minecraft!
NFT’s: Purchase a plot of land, sell it on the market. Purchase a sweet skin, put it on the market. Minecraft + Crypto = Monetized in game items on steroids.
